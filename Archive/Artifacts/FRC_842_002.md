## Executive Summary
This paper documents the 13-stage evolution of Project Chimera. It serves as a comprehensive reference for why **Fixed Non-Gated Reservoirs** are insufficient for high-dimensional classification tasks compared to plastic, gated networks. We map the journey from the "Plasticity Paradox" to "Definitive Ablation."

## 1. The Plasticity Paradox (V1 - V4)
Initial experiments used Liquid State Machines (LSM) with fixed encoders.
- **Finding**: While stable, the system lacked the discriminative power to separate digits without catastrophic loss in the readout layer.
